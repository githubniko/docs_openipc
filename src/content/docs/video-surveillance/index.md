OpenIPC is first of all firmware for IP cameras and video surveillance. Start here when your goal is to turn a supported camera into a regular network camera for RTSP, ONVIF, NVR, or Majestic-based operation.

Other sections such as FPV, OpenWrt, home automation, and streaming are practical applications built on top of the same camera platform. They are useful, but they do not replace the standard video surveillance path.

## Start with video surveillance

1. Check that your camera SoC and flash layout are supported.
2. Install OpenIPC firmware using the matching recovery or flashing guide.
3. Complete the first boot and password setup.
4. Configure Majestic, video streams, network access, and recording or NVR integration.
5. Use the advanced use cases only after the camera works as a stable IP camera.
